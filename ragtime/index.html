01. Introduction

Months of hard work, long evenings, and meticulous rehearsals all come together on a fleeting few shows at the end of the term. It's an extremely rewarding experience for all cast and crew involved. Andover's Fall 2017 student musical production of the massive and epic 3-hour-long "Ragtime" was no different, and I had the privilege of capturing moments that happened onstage. Here are those pictures.

04. Conclusion

I've been involved with my school's musicals for several years now, spanning from New York City to Massachusetts. My roles have included pianist, keyboardist, percussionist, and photographer. "Ragtime" was my introduction to the expansive theater and dance department at Andover, and it was an amazing one, full of excitement and emotion and support. I'm so glad to have had the opportunity to be involved, and look forward to a future of similar collaborations.
