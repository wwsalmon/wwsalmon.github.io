This portfolio and all of its project pages are under construction. They may not accurately represent me or my work. The portfolio and all of its case studies will be completed in the near future; I will send out an email to the applicable people when the portfolio is complete.

Four years ago, my friends and I sawed an old apple crate in half, attached it to the legs of an old telescope, and made a tripod for my old iPad. That's how my passion for filmmaking started.

Since then, I have made three narrative short films, multiple documentary/promotional films as the media director for my robotics team, and have experience with color grading, sound design, and visual effects in Premiere Pro and After Effects.
