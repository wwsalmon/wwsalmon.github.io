My Story
Five years ago, my friends and I sawed an old apple crate in half, attached it to the legs of an old telescope, and made a tripod for my old iPad.

My name is Samson Zhang. I live in New York City and am an eleventh grader at Phillips Academy in Andover, MA.

I love storytelling, especially through film and video. I've worked on all kinds of projects, from 30-second shorts to 30-minute documentaries, including narrative shorts, music videos, YouTube videos, and promotional campaign videos.

My philosophy is "do to learn, not learn to do." I'm constantly experimenting and learning new things. Other than filmmaking and photography, I love UI/UX design, web design and development, running, cycling, playing piano, geeking out about tech, and Rocket League.
